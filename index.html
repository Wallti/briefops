Process

Four steps.
No manual work.

01
Submit your meeting
Paste a transcript, upload audio, or forward a memo. BriefOps accepts anything — structured or not.
02
Tasks extracted
The parser identifies every action item, assigns owners from your org-chart, and detects cross-team dependencies automatically.
03
Approval & routing
Team leads review a structured summary before anything is written to Jira or sent to Slack. One click to approve the full batch.
04
Delivered & tracked
Tasks land in your tools. The follow-up agent monitors deadlines and escalates automatically when nothing moves.
